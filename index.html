School Assist streamlines parent, teacher, and student interaction into a mobile solution to help parents stay engaged what exactly what's occuring in their chidlren's classroom. The platform is deliverd via a web portal that will also serve as a source of information, communication, and collaboration for parents, educators, and students to help foster a culture of excellence in education. The first iteration of parent assist will feature a blend of easy to use Social Media Mashups to address the lack of parental invovlment in the academics for middle school and highschool students. Future versions of School Assist will help also tackle the reading epedemic in this country.

Lack of Parental Involvement

School Assist will provide a unified means for parents, educators, and students to communicate and collaborate via direct messages, blogs, and online video conference. Parents will also be able to access the Student Weekend feature which will give them insight into the student's classroom performance that week along with what the student should review over the weekend.

The Reading Epedemic

One in four children grow up without learning how to read. More than one-third of fourth-graders are unable to complete schoolwork successfully because they struggle with reading. School Assist will deliver eLearning reading material to middle school students.
